Our mission is to overcome the language barrier beween teachers and their ESL students.

The Covid-19 Pandemic has made learning for ESL students extremely difficult. Many find it very hard, almost impossible, to understand what their teachers are teaching in an online environment. However, English is a much-needed language in today's world. It is the language of academia, as most post-secondary education classes are taught in English, even in countries where it is not the native language. That is why we created enline. enline is a simple to use online platform that allows ESL teachers to upload their lessons onto our cloud and translate them to their students' preferred languages. English is a complex language to learn, and we believe the learning experience should be made as easy as possible for all students.
